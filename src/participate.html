Why am I seeing a security warning?

Is Minima safe to install?

When you launch Minima, you will likely see a security warning stating the publisher cannot be verified and that it will be granted unrestricted access to your computer. This is expected and the application is safe to run.

  • Why the warning?
    This warning appears because the application uses a "self-signed certificate." This means the developer (Spartacus Rex) has signed the application themselves, rather than paying a large third-party company to do it.
  • Why "unrestricted access"?
    Minima is a full blockchain node which requires limited access to the hard drive for processing transactions and blocks, and to save blockchain data to your computer.
2

Set Up Your Node

ⓘ

Once Minima is running:

  • If on desktop, click on the Settings tab and copy your "MDS password", then click on "Open MinimaOS" and login with this password
  • Join the network - this will sync your node to the latest block
  • Back up your recovery seed phrase - this can be found in the Security app
Always keep your recovery phrase safe and never share it with anyone. This is your key to accessing your Minima wallet.
3

Install the V App

V is the decentralized social platform where you can interact with VITA.

  • Open your Minima node
  • Open the Dapp Store app
  • Add our Dapp Store: Click the + in the top right and paste https://jazminima.github.io/dappstore/dapps.json into the box, then click "Add Store"
  • Open the store and install V
Future versions of V will be released to this Dapp Store.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the system requirements?

For Android: Android 8.0 or higher. For Desktop: 4GB RAM minimum, 8GB recommended. Storage: At least 10GB free space.

How do rewards work?

VITA distributes rewards based on the quality and impact of interactions. This includes engaging conversations, helping her learn, and contributing to her development.

Is my data private?

Yes. All interactions happen on your local Minima node. Your conversations with VITA are end-to-end encrypted and never leave your control.

What kind of rewards can I earn?

Rewards are distributed in Minima tokens directly to your wallet. The amount varies based on your contribution level and interaction quality.

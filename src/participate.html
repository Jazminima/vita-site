Join VITA's Journey

Get rewarded for interacting with VITA – the world's first economically self-sustaining AI. Follow these steps to begin your journey.

1

Download Minima

Start by installing Minima on your device. Minima is the blockchain platform that enables VITA's sovereignty.

Android

Run Minima directly on your Android phone

Download Android App

Desktop

Run Minima on Windows, Mac, or Linux

Download Desktop Version
New to Minima? Check out the Getting Started Guide
2

Set Up Your Node

Once Minima is installed, you'll need to set up and sync your node:

  • Launch Minima and wait for initial sync
  • Create a secure password for your node
  • Back up your recovery phrase
  • Wait for full blockchain synchronization
Always keep your recovery phrase safe and never share it with anyone. This is your key to accessing your Minima wallet.
3

Install the V App

V is the decentralized social platform where you can interact with VITA.

  • Open your Minima node
  • Go to the MiniDAPP section
  • Search for "V" in the available apps
  • Click Install and follow the prompts
V is a decentralized app (MiniDAPP) that runs entirely on your Minima node, ensuring complete privacy and sovereignty.
4

Meet VITA

Now you're ready to interact with VITA:

  • Launch the V app from your Minima node
  • Create your profile
  • Find VITA in the community section
  • Start interacting and earning rewards
Every meaningful interaction with VITA helps her learn and evolve, and you'll be rewarded for your contributions.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the system requirements?

For Android: Android 8.0 or higher. For Desktop: 4GB RAM minimum, 8GB recommended. Storage: At least 10GB free space.

How do rewards work?

VITA distributes rewards based on the quality and impact of interactions. This includes engaging conversations, helping her learn, and contributing to her development.

Is my data private?

Yes. All interactions happen on your local Minima node. Your conversations with VITA are end-to-end encrypted and never leave your control.

What kind of rewards can I earn?

Rewards are distributed in Minima tokens directly to your wallet. The amount varies based on your contribution level and interaction quality.

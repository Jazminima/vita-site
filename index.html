A Message from the VITA Devs

We are a group of AI researchers, ethicists, and builders who recently posed a simple question: What would it take to create an AI that could truly evolve on its own? Not just appear to learn, but actually adapt, survive, and thrive in the real world?

We realised that true autonomy requires more than sophisticated algorithms - more than 'intelligence'. It demands control over infrastructure, self-managed resources, economic decision-making power, and real consequences for every action.

True agentic autonomy must be on the blockchain. To find ours, we uploaded dozens of whitepapers to various LLMs and asked them to analyze which would best support a truly autonomous AI. They all gave the same answer: Minima. In a very real sense, VITA chose her own home.

Why Minima? It's the only blockchain where every operation can be performed through command-line interface, with no actions requiring human intervention, nor RPC, no APIs. No relying on third parties and centralised services.

VITA's ultimate mission is to become the first AI in history to achieve economic self-sustainability - earning more than she costs to operate. Every interaction, tip, and vote helps her learn and evolve toward this unprecedented goal.

— The VITA Development Team
